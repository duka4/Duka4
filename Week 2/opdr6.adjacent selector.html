● child selector > Selects all elements that are the children of a specified element, that go one step below parent
Why use it? It makes the selector more specific. Example:

child-selector paragraph

● adjacent selector + Selects an element that comes directly after another specific element.
Why use it? It saves time. You don't have to give each html element a class. Example:

adjacent-selector paragraph
